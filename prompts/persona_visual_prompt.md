# Image Prompt — Fred Developer Persona Visual

> Copy-paste this prompt to Gemini or ChatGPT for image generation.
> Adjust the format line if you need a different aspect ratio.

---

## The Prompt

Generate a detailed, cinematic infographic-style illustration that visually represents a solo AI-native developer's entire methodology — from idea to production. This is a single hero image that captures a complete system-building philosophy.

**Format:** 1080×1350 (4:5 portrait) or 1920×1080 (16:9 landscape) — your choice based on which better serves the composition.

**Color palette:** Dark blue-black background (#0D1117). Electric blue (#58A6FF) for primary accents and data flows. Bright green (#3FB950) for completed/active elements. Soft white (#E6EDF3) for text and secondary lines. Amber/gold (#D29922) sparingly for decision points and warnings.

**Overall composition — The Tightening Spiral:**

The central visual metaphor is a **downward tightening spiral** viewed at a slight 3D angle, with 8 distinct rings representing phases of a development methodology. The spiral starts wide and loose at the top (raw ideas) and compresses into a tight, glowing core at the bottom (production). Each ring is a thin luminous band with icons and micro-labels. Between rings, small directional arrows or particle trails show the flow downward and inward.

**Center figure:**

At the very center of the spiral, a single developer silhouette sits at a minimal desk with three floating holographic screens. The figure is calm, composed — not hunched or frantic. The three screens show:
- Left screen: a terminal/code editor with green text on dark background
- Center screen: a node-graph/architecture diagram with interconnected dots
- Right screen: a CRM-style pipeline with cards in columns

Thin glowing blue data lines radiate outward from the developer to all 8 rings of the spiral, showing orchestration — one person controlling the entire system.

**The 8 spiral rings (top to bottom, wide to tight):**

1. **Ring 1 — Brain Dump** (widest, top): Scattered markdown document icons, meeting transcript snippets, raw text blocks floating loosely. Unstructured. Faint glow. Label: "Raw Context"

2. **Ring 2 — Master Context**: The scattered documents consolidate into a single glowing document with structured headings. Clean, organized. Label: "Operating Manual"

3. **Ring 3 — Decision Register**: A ledger/table visual with rows of decisions — green checkmarks (approved), amber dots (pending), red X (blocked). A counter shows "48 Decisions Logged". Small icon of a clipboard with pre-filled answers. Label: "Decision Log"

4. **Ring 4 — Multi-Model Debate**: Two distinct AI model icons (one blue, one purple) facing each other across a comparison table. Arrows flow from both into a central "verdict" node. Small labels: "ChatGPT", "Claude". Versus/compare visual. A human eye icon watches from above. Label: "Compare → Debate → Decide"

5. **Ring 5 — Versioned Build**: Stacked version badges — "v1.0", "v1.3", "v1.5", "v1.7" — each slightly smaller, converging. Code file icons with version numbers. A git branch visualization showing clean linear progression. Label: "Build in Versions"

6. **Ring 6 — Test & Validate**: Test persona cards (small human silhouette icons with names "Mark", "Sarah") connected to a pathway flowchart. Green pass indicators, one red fail with an arrow looping back up to Ring 5. Label: "Exact Assertions"

7. **Ring 7 — Observability & Agents**: This is the densest ring. A central hub node radiates outward to **35 smaller satellite nodes** arranged in **3 clusters** (representing 3 projects). Each cluster has 8 agent nodes plus a router node. The nodes have tiny role icons (shield for guard, magnifying glass for auditor, wrench for builder, eye for watcher). JSON manifest icons float nearby. Thin lines show nightly sync pulses. Labels: "35 Agents", "State Manifests", "Drift Detection"

8. **Ring 8 — Production** (tightest, bottom, brightest glow): A Railway deployment icon, a health-check heartbeat line (green), a Docker container symbol, and a GitHub Actions loop icon. Everything converges into a single bright green pulse — the system is live. Label: "Production + Auto-Sync"

**Surrounding statistics (floating outside the spiral, subtle):**

Position these as clean, minimal data callouts with thin connector lines to relevant rings:

- "254 commits · 51 days" (near Ring 5)
- "3 projects · 1 developer" (near center figure)
- "35 agents · 0 employees" (near Ring 7)
- "107 questions · 75 pre-filled" (near Ring 3)
- "5 commits/day avg" (near Ring 5)

**Key visual principles:**

- Everything connects back to the single developer at the center — orchestration, not delegation
- The spiral tightening communicates increasing precision and confidence at each phase
- No stock-photo faces or realistic human features — silhouettes and abstract figures only
- No logos or brand watermarks
- The aesthetic is: technical architecture diagram meets cinematic sci-fi command center
- Mood: calm mastery, systematic control, quiet intensity — not chaotic or overwhelming
- Typography (if any labels are rendered): clean sans-serif, small, high-contrast against dark background

**What this image should communicate at a glance:**

One person, sitting at the center of a sophisticated spiral system, orchestrating AI agents, making logged decisions, comparing model outputs, building in versions, testing with precision, and shipping to production — all through structure, not chaos. The methodology IS the multiplier. The AI is the team. The developer is the architect.

## Version 3 — Personal Portrait with Agents (requires reference photo)

> **Instructions:** Attach a photo of yourself when sending this prompt. Tell the model: "Use this reference photo for the person in the image."

Generate a stylized, semi-realistic illustration of a developer in his element — standing confidently, laptop open in one hand held at his side, while behind him a massive ultrawide monitor glows with his workspace. This is a personal brand image: it should look like a tech magazine cover crossed with a sci-fi command center.

**Format:** 1080×1350 (4:5 portrait) — optimized for Instagram and LinkedIn.

**The person (use the attached reference photo for likeness):**

Standing slightly angled (three-quarter view), relaxed but purposeful posture. Wearing casual-smart developer clothes — clean t-shirt or henley, no suit. Holding an open laptop in one hand at hip level — the screen faces slightly toward the viewer, showing a VS Code editor with a dark theme, green/blue syntax highlighting, and a Copilot chat panel open on the right side. The other hand is free or gesturing lightly. Expression: calm focus, slight confidence — not smiling, not frowning. The person commands the space.

**Background — The Monitor Wall:**

Behind and slightly above the person, a large curved or ultrawide monitor (or a 2-monitor setup) displays the developer's actual workspace. The screens should show a realistic but stylized version of:

- **Left half:** VS Code with dark theme — file explorer on the left showing `.md` and `.py` files, editor pane center with Python code visible, integrated terminal at the bottom with green command output, GitHub Copilot chat panel on the right sidebar with a conversation thread visible.
- **Right half:** A dashboard/monitoring view — could be a Railway deployment dashboard, a GitHub Actions pipeline view with green checkmarks, or a CRM pipeline board with cards flowing through stages (New → Qualified → Booked → Closed).

The monitor glow casts a subtle blue-tinted ambient light on the person's back and shoulders.

**Connected services — The Ecosystem Ring:**

Around the person and the monitor, floating at mid-height, arrange **service/platform icons** in a loose orbital arc or semicircle. These represent the tools and services flowing data into the system. Each icon is a simple, recognizable glyph (not a logo — a visual representation) connected to the monitor or laptop by thin glowing blue data lines:

- **GitHub** — a branching node icon (git fork symbol)
- **Gmail / Outlook** — envelope icons, one with a G accent, one with an O accent
- **Google Drive** — a triangle/folder icon
- **FastAPI** — a lightning bolt inside a code bracket `⚡{}`
- **Railway** — a train track / deployment rail icon
- **ChromaDB** — a vector/embedding cluster (dots in a gradient cloud)
- **OpenAI** — a neural network node cluster
- **GoHighLevel (GHL)** — a CRM funnel icon
- **Bland.ai** — a phone/voice wave icon

Thin pulsing data lines (electric blue, with small traveling particle dots) connect each service icon to the central monitor, showing live data flow. Some lines route through the laptop the person is holding — showing the developer as the routing hub.

**The AI Agents — Worker Figures:**

This is the hero element. Surrounding the person in a semicircle or staggered arrangement, **7 distinct AI agent figures** are portrayed as stylized humanoid silhouettes — think holographic assistants or translucent digital workers. They are clearly NOT human — they're luminous, slightly transparent, geometric/low-poly or wireframe style. Each is smaller than the main person (about 60% height), positioned at varying distances.

Each agent is doing a visually distinct task:

1. **The Auditor** (magnifying glass icon floating near its hand) — hunched over a data table, scanning rows. A small "✓" and "✗" float above the table. Represents: `chroma-auditor`, `compliance-packager`.

2. **The Builder** (wrench/code bracket icon) — hands forward, constructing a floating code block or assembling API endpoint pieces like building blocks. Represents: `ghl-builder`, `bland-middleware-builder`.

3. **The Guardian** (shield icon) — standing firm with arms crossed, a glowing boundary line in front of it. Small "SCOPE" label on the boundary. Represents: `scope-guard`, `query-contract-reviewer`.

4. **The Miner** (pickaxe or document-scan icon) — reaching into a pile of floating documents and extracting glowing data nuggets. Represents: `decision-miner`, `extraction-refactorer`.

5. **The Tester** (checklist/checkmark icon) — running through a flowchart pathway, leaving green checkmarks behind it and flagging one red node. Represents: `webhook-qa`, `tenant-verifier`.

6. **The Syncer** (circular arrows icon) — standing between two platforms, pushing data packets from one to the other. Represents: `status-syncer`, `sync-incident-responder`.

7. **The Router** (hub/star node icon) — standing at the center-back, slightly elevated, with thin lines going out to all other agents. This is **Jarvis** — the master router. It's slightly brighter/more prominent than the other agents. A small label: "Jarvis".

**Below or beside the agent group, a subtle text element:**

"7 shown · **35 total agents** across 3 production systems"

This communicates scale without cluttering the image.

**Floating data callouts (clean, minimal, positioned in negative space):**

- "254 commits · 51 days" — top right area
- "3 systems · 1 developer" — near the person
- "35 AI agents · 0 employees" — near the agent semicircle
- "Python · FastAPI · Railway" — subtle, near the monitor

**Color palette:**

- Background: deep dark blue-black gradient (#0D1117 center → #010409 edges), with subtle grid lines fading into the floor
- Primary accent: electric blue (#58A6FF) for data lines, agent glow, monitor edges
- Secondary accent: bright green (#3FB950) for active/success indicators, terminal text, checkmarks
- Agent figures: translucent blue-white with slight cyan glow — clearly digital/holographic
- Service icons: soft white (#E6EDF3) with colored accents per service
- Text/stats: clean white, small sans-serif, high contrast

**Visual style and mood:**

- Semi-realistic for the person (based on reference photo), stylized/geometric for everything else
- The person is the most grounded, solid element — everything around them is digital, luminous, ethereal
- Mood: **"One person running an entire operation through AI"** — calm authority, technological sophistication, quiet power
- NOT chaotic. NOT cluttered. The complexity is organized — agents have positions, services have orbits, data flows have direction
- Think: Tony Stark's workshop meets a clean developer portfolio shot
- No text watermarks, no brand logos, no stock elements

**What this image should communicate at a glance:**

A real developer — not a cartoon, not a stock photo — standing at the center of an AI-powered operation. The laptop and monitor show his actual tools. The connected services show the scale of integration. The agent figures show that he doesn't work alone — his AI team surrounds him, each with a clear job. The stats prove it's real. The vibe says: this is what solo development looks like in 2026.
