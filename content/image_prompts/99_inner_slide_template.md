# Inner Slide Template Prompt

Generate a 1080x1350 Instagram carousel inner-slide template.

Requirements:
- Dark background (`#0D1117`)
- Thin vertical accent bar on the left
- Step number top-left (e.g., `03`)

- Deliver three variants with accent colors: blue, green, purple

Goal: text-first layout that stays highly readable on mobile.
