Turns out, they require a selfie, three AI tools, and a system.

The breakthrough wasn't generating the caricature. It was asking AI to ANALYZE it — break down what makes my face recognizable, what anchors my identity, what must stay consistent.

That analysis became my master prompt.

ChatGPT generated the caricature. ChatGPT analyzed the identity. Claude structured the reusable prompt system. Gemini generates all scenes from it.

Three AIs, each doing what it's best at. That's the methodology.
