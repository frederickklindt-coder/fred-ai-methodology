# 09 - From No-Code to AI Systems

## Topic
How I went from no-code developer to building AI production systems in one month — after getting laid off.

## Audience
No-code/low-code developers wondering how to level up with AI. Builders at a crossroads. Anyone who's felt fear during a career shift.

## Result
A real hero-journey transition story — emotional + practical — with concrete steps and proof.

## CTA Keyword
`SHIFT`

---

## Slides

### Slide 1 — Hook
2 months ago I was laid off.

My boss and I had different visions for AI.

He wanted to adapt one way.
I was already going another.

One of us was wrong.

I decided to find out which.

### Slide 2 — The Fall
I felt lost. Scared.

4 years of Bubble, product management, QA.
Good at building fast.

But suddenly — no job. No team.
AI revolution happening at full speed.

The ceiling wasn't just clear anymore.
It had come crashing down.

### Slide 3 — The Reset
I gave myself one week.

No job hunting. No panic applying.
Just: breathe. Learn. Think.

I put my mind in the right place first.
Then I made the jump.

### Slide 4 — Where I Started
No-code taught me something valuable:
systems matter more than syntax.

4 years of Bubble forced me to think in flows,
in states, in user journeys.

That mental model? It transferred.

### Slide 5 — The Shift
I stopped learning tools.
I started designing systems.

AI became infrastructure, not a feature.

Before: drag-and-drop → deploy → hope.
After: context → prompt → debate → build → version → iterate.

### Slide 6 — The Stack
Python. FastAPI. Railway.
ChromaDB. OpenAI. GitHub.

Not random — each piece chosen for a reason.

No CS degree. Just systems thinking + AI as a copilot.

### Slide 7 — Myth Break
You don't need a CS degree to build AI systems.

You need systems thinking.

No-code taught me exactly that.
The skill transferred.
The ceiling didn't come with me.

### Slide 8 — Proof
In ~2 months after the layoff:
→ 3 production systems deployed
→ 35 AI agents built and running
→ $4K/month generated for a client
→ AI ops pipelines, CRM automation, white-label SaaS
→ All solo. All production.

The fear didn't go away.
I built through it anyway.

### Slide 9 — CTA
AI can replace the 40 hours of busywork.
It can't replace your story, your pivot, or your will to rebuild.

That's the real moat.
