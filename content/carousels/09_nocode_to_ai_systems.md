## Topic
How I went from no-code developer to building AI production systems in one month.

## Audience
No-code/low-code developers wondering how to level up with AI.

## Result
A real transition story with practical steps.

## CTA Keyword
`SHIFT`

## Slides

### Slide 1 - Hook
I had 1 month to reinvent how I build.

From no-code dev → AI systems.
Now generating $4K/month for a client.

Revenue-generating client work.

You need systems thinking.
No-code taught me that.
