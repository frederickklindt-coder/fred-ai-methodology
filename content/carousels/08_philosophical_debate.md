# 08 - Philosophical AI Debate

## Topic
Using AI as a thinking partner, not just an answer machine.

## Audience
Curious minds who want to go deeper with AI.

## Result
A new way to use AI — for friction, not just answers.

## CTA Keyword
`THINK`

## Slides

### Slide 1 - Hook
I had a debate with AI about free will.

It didn't agree with me.
That's when it got interesting.

### Slide 2 - The Setup
I didn't ask a question.
I stated a position.
Then I asked the AI to challenge it.

### Slide 3 - The Exchange
It argued we are predictable systems.
I argued for human unpredictability.
Then it flipped the argument on me.

### Slide 4 - The Value
AI debates are not about being right.
They're about pressure-testing your thinking.

### Slide 5 - The Method
1. State your position clearly
2. Ask AI to steelman the opposite
3. Challenge its assumptions
4. Let it challenge yours
5. Extract what survived

### Slide 6 - Why It Works
Most people use AI for answers.
Builders use AI for friction.

Friction sharpens thinking.
Comfort dulls it.

### Slide 7 - Myth Break
AI is not just a tool for productivity.
It's a thinking partner —
if you stop asking it to agree with you.

### Slide 8 - Proof
These debates became a podcast concept.
Debate → transcript → script → audio.
Content from thinking, not templates.

### Slide 9 - CTA
AI can replace the 40 hours of busywork.
It can't replace the questions only a human would think to ask.

That's what makes these debates worth having.

Want to hear an AI debate?
Comment `THINK`.
