# 01 - Solo System Builder

## Topic
How I ship production systems solo with AI.

## Audience
Builders curious about AI leverage and systems thinking.

## Result
A repeatable framework from idea to production.

## CTA Keyword
`SYSTEM`

## Slides

### Slide 1 - Hook
I shipped 3 production systems in 51 days.

No team.
No funding.
No burnout.

### Slide 2 - Problem
Most people use AI to write code.

AI is not a typist.
AI is an engineering team (if you structure it).

### Slide 3 - Step 1
I start with a 200-line operating manual.

Business context.
Architecture.
Constraints.
Decisions needed.

### Slide 4 - Step 2
Before build:
- Map blockers.
- Pre-fill ~70% of answers.
- Send one approval batch.

### Slide 5 - Step 3
Same prompt to ChatGPT and Claude.

Compare.
Debate.
Pick strongest reasoning per section.

### Slide 6 - Step 4
When a task repeats 3+ times, I build an agent:
- Job description
- Startup reads
- Write scope
- Guardrails

### Slide 7 - Myth Break
AI did not replace me.
AI replaced my need for a larger team.
I still own all decisions.

### Slide 8 - Proof
- 254 commits in 51 days
- 3 production systems
- 35 specialist AI agents
- Nightly auto-sync
- 48 logged decisions
